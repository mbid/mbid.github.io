Martin E. Bidlingmaier

I'm a Senior Software Engineer at Nvidia. Previously, I worked at Google and completed a PhD with Bas Spitters at the Logic and Semantics Group at Aarhus University.

While I'm not an active academic anymore, I'm still exploring ideas in programming languages, for example dependent type theory, categorical semantics and Datalog. You can check out my blog posts and list of publications below. If you're looking for help on projects related to programming languages, please reach out.

You can reach me via email at martin.bidlingmaier@mbid.me.
